Meet the grills

Small

Small enough to take with you, pretty much anywhere, with the ability to grill up just about anything. Good times and food await you in this small package. Serves 6.

  • Diameter: 14 sq. inches
  • Fuel Type: Charcoal
  • Plated steel cooking grate

Medium

Ignite the coals and grill for the entire family on this classic charcoal grill. Plenty of space for grilling up a bunch of steaks or a burger for anyone who stops by. Serves 5-7.

  • Diameter: 26 sq. inches
  • Fuel Type: Charcoal
  • Wheels and tool hooks

The Beast

The extra large grilling area, six burners, abundance of features, and optimal storage space leave you questioning a need for an "indoor kitchen" one dinner at a time. Prep, grill, and serve outside where the party is at. Serves 8+.

  • Diameter: 624 sq. inches
  • Fuel Type: Gas
  • Shelves, cabinet, stainless steel everything
